About ACT North Campus

🎓

Excellence in Education

Committed to providing world-class technical education that prepares students for the challenges of tomorrow's digital world.

Innovation Hub

Industry Partnerships

Strong connections with leading technology companies ensuring our graduates are industry-ready from day one.

We pride ourselves on nurturing talented individuals who go on to become leaders in technology, engineering, and innovation. Our comprehensive curriculum, experienced faculty, and modern facilities create an environment where students thrive and achieve their full potential.
